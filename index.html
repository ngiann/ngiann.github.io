Hi there, this is my personal research page

My name is Nikos Gianniotis and I am a researcher at the Astroinformatics Group, at the Heidelberg Institute for Theoretical Studies (HITS).

I work on applications of machine learning in astronomy. My research interests revolve around the topics of dimensionality reduction, Bayesian inference and probabilistic modelling.
